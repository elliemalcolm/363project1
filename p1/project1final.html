How To Assemble Pasta

This Week’s Recipe For College Students: Penne Pasta with Marinara Sauce
Bring two cups of water to a boil, then add in one serving of penne pasta. Drain once softened to desired consistency
Take half of store bought marinara sauce and pour into sauce pan on medium heat, stirring frequently until cooked pasta is complete
Combine cooked pasta and heated marinara sauce in sauce pan, adding in italian seasoning and grated parmesan cheese for flavor. Serve and enjoy!
